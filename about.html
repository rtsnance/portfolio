01 — The Path
Writing, teaching,
then design

Twenty‑one years of experience design, built on a decade of words.

My background spans writing, linguistics, architecture, fine arts, and literature. Before design, I taught for eight years and edited for four — work that shaped how I practice: a bias toward clarity, an ear for how people actually talk about what they need, and the patience to build understanding before building screens.

For the last twenty‑one years I've been an experience designer — a creative problem solver with the ability to articulate, clearly and compellingly, a vision of a surprising and successful user experience.

Across consumer marketplaces, e‑commerce, and analytics SaaS, I've set strategy with C‑suites, built and mentored teams, stood up design systems, and run the research that changes what gets built.
